RECIPE TO MAKE MILK


DIRECTION FOR USE:


To mix 1 Litre
1、Add 1 cups (100%) of instant Golden Koala®Skim Milk Powder to 2 cups of hot or cold water.
2、Stir until dissolved.

3、Add more water to make up 1 litre.


To mix 1 cup:
1、Add 1/4 cups (25g) of instant Golden Koala®Skim Milk Powder to 1/2 cups of hot or cold water.
2、Stir until dissolved.

3、Add more water to make up 1 cup.


For best results always add powder to water.
For richer flavour and extra nourishment, use additional powder to suit your personal taste or preference To enhance the natural flavour of this product we recommend milk in the refrigerator overnight prior to consumption.
Golden koala® Skim Milk Powder can also be used in cooking to make many wonderful dishes.
